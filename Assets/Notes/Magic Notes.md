A hybrid approach:
There are magic weapons that deal magic damage in player phase and enemy phase but consume MP per attack. Equipping certain weapons/accessories may grant an additional spell to the user.

There are 5 categories of weapons right now: Sword / Lance / Blunt / Ranged / Magic

I added this because combat arts are empowered weapon attacks, and it wouldn't make sense for a sword to use a "Snipe shot" combat art or a "heavy slam" combat art. I wonder if there is a more robust distinction though. For example, is a polearm a light melee or heavy melee weapon? Does it make sense for it to access slashing combat arts or blunt force combat arts. Maybe it needs a separate category? What is it named? Or maybe it can just be split into Light and Heavy Melee depending on the size of the polearm? I'm not sure.
